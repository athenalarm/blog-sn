## II. Zvitupa Zvikuru Zvinofanira Kuve Neumwe Nemumwe Mugoveri weMidziyo yeBurglar Alarm

1. [ISO 9001](https://www.iso.org/iso-9001-quality-management.html) – Masisitimu eKutarisira Hunhu

Chii icho:
ISO 9001 inosimbisa kuti sisitimu yekutarisira hunhu (QMS) yemugoveri inosangana nemisimboti inozivikanwa pasi rose, ichitarisa kuenderana kwemaitiro, kuvandudzwa kunoenderera, uye kugutsikana kwevatengi.

Sei zvichikosha:
Kune vatengi vanotenga muhuwandu, ISO 9001 inoratidza maitiro ekugadzira akarongeka. Vagoveri vane ISO 9001 vanowanzo ratidza:

* Maitiro akaenderana ekuedzwa kwemabasa.
* Kuteedzerwa kwakasimba mukugadzira nekuchinja kwemadhizaini.
* Mwero wakaderera wekukanganisa uye kuita kwakavimbika.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Zvikwata zvekutenga zvinogona kutarisira mhando yechigadzirwa inofanotaurika, yakakosha pakuiswa kukuru uko kuenderana kwemazana emayuniti kwakakosha.

2. [CE Marking](https://ec.europa.eu/growth/single-market/ce-marking_en) – Kuenderana kweEurope

Chii icho:
CE marking inoratidza kuti zvigadzirwa zvinoenderana nemirairo yeEU yezvehutano, kuchengetedzwa, uye zvakatipoteredza, kusanganisira kuenderana kwemagetsi uye kuchengetedzwa kwemagetsi akaderera.

Sei zvichikosha:
Chiratidzo cheCE chinodiwa pakutengesa masisitimu eburglar alarm muEuropean Economic Area (EEA).

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Vatengi vanonanga musika weEU vanodzivirira kunonoka kwetsika uye nyaya dzemutoro sezvo zvigadzirwa zvine CE zvapfuura kuongororwa kwemitemo. Izvi zvinonyanya kukosha kumapaneli ealarm ane waya kana asina waya uye zvishandiso zvine chekuita nawo.

3. [UL Standards](https://www.ul.com/services/standards-certification) – Chitupa Chekuchengetedzwa cheNorth America

Chii icho:
Mitemo yeUnderwriters Laboratories (UL) ndeimwe yezvitupa zvinoremekedzwa zvikuru zvekuchengetedzwa kwemagetsi nezvigadzirwa zvealarm muU.S. neCanada.

Mitemo yeUL yakakosha inosanganisira:

* UL 681 – Masisitimu eburglar alarm.
* UL 827 – Mabasa ecentral-station alarm.

Sei zvichikosha:
Chitupa cheUL chinopa vimbiso yakazvimirira yekuti masisitimu anosangana nezvinodiwa zvakasimba zvekuchengetedzwa kwemagetsi uye mashandiro.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Vatengi vanotenga muhuwandu muNorth America vanobatsirwa nekuderedzwa kwemari yeinishuwarenzi uye njodzi dzemutemo pakushandisa midziyo ine chitupa cheUL.

4. [FCC Certification](https://www.fcc.gov/engineering-technology/electromagnetic-compatibility-division/equipment-authorization) – Kuenderana kweElectromagnetic (US)

Chii icho:
Federal Communications Commission (FCC) inodzora midziyo inoshandisa radio frequency. Izvi zvinosanganisira vatakuri nevagamuchiri veburglar alarm vasina waya.

Sei zvichikosha:
Mvumo yeFCC inovimbisa kuti kutaurirana kwealarm pasina waya hakukanganisi mamwe masisitimu akakosha uye kunoenderana nemiganhu yekuburitswa kweRF.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Vatengi vanoshandisa mapuratifomu ealarm eGSM/4G/Wi-Fi muNorth America vanowana chokwadi chekuti masaini achashanda zviri pamutemo uye zvakavimbika pasina kukanganisa kusina kutarisirwa.

5. [EN 50131](https://standards.iteh.ai/catalog/standards/clc/3f3d3e83-2b0a-4a5b-9a2a-9b3b5a0d4f6e/en-50131-1-2006) – Mureza weEurope weIntrusion & Hold-Up Alarm

Chii icho:
EN 50131 imureza wakazara wealarm unoshandiswa muEurope yose, unotsanangura mazinga ekugiredha (Giredhi 1–4), nzira dzekuyedza, uye zviyero zvekuita.

Sei zvichikosha:
Kugiredha kunotaura zvakananga nezvemwero wenjodzi uye kuita kwekuchengetedzwa.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Kune kuiswa kwakakosha—senge nzvimbo dze data, nhandare dzendege, kana masangano emari—masisitimu ane giredhi repamusoro (semuenzaniso Giredhi 3 kana Giredhi 4) anopa vatengi chivimbo chekuti midziyo inosangana nemiganhu yakasimba yekushanda.

6. Mitemo Yezvakatipoteredza – [RoHS](https://environment.ec.europa.eu/topics/waste-and-recycling/rohs-directive_en) & [REACH](https://echa.europa.eu/regulations/reach/understanding-reach)

Zvii zvazviri:

* RoHS inodzora kushandiswa kwezvinhu zvine ngozi muzvigadzirwa zvemagetsi.
* REACH inodzora makemikari uye kushandiswa kwawo kwakachengeteka muEU.

Sei zvichikosha:
Kutevedzera zvakatipoteredza kuri kuwedzera kusungirirwa kumitemo yekutenga kwemakambani inotarisa pakusimudzira.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Vatengi vanotevedzera zvinangwa zveEnvironmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) vanobatsirwa nekushanda nevagoveri vane zvikamu nemaitiro ekugadzira anoderedza zvinhu zvine ngozi.

7. [CCC](https://www.ccc-mark.com/) – China Compulsory Certification

Chii icho:
CCC chitupa chekuchengetedzwa nemhando chinomanikidzwa muChina.

Sei zvichikosha:
Vagoveri vanogadzira muChina vanofanira kutevedzera CCC kuti vatengese zviri pamutemo mukati menyika uye kazhinji kuratidza kutevedzera mumaketani ekupa eAsia-Pacific.

Mhedzisiro chaiyo:
Vanoisa zvinhu kunze kwenyika pasi rose, kunyanya vanotenga muhuwandu kubva kuChina, vanoona kuti kutevedzera CCC kunobatsira kufambisa mitemo yeAsia-Pacific.

## III. Misiyano Yematunhu muMitemo uye Matambudziko eKutevedzera

Europe

* Nzvimbo yemitemo inosanganisira CE, EN 50131, uye mitemo yekuvanzika kwedata (semuenzaniso [GDPR](https://gdpr.eu/)) apo masisitimu ealarm anobatanidza network kana cloud services.
* Mushure meBrexit, [UKCA marking](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/using-the-ukca-marking) iri kutsiva zvinodiwa zveCE pakupinda mumusika weUK.

North America

* Mitemo yeUL neFCC inotsanangura hwaro hwakawanda hwemitemo, ichitsigirwa nemakodhi ekuvaka nekuchengetedzwa ematunhu nemunharaunda.
* Vatengi vekuCanada vanofanira kuongorora zvakaenzana zve[ULC](https://canada.ul.com/ulcstandards/) (Underwriters Laboratories of Canada).

Asia-Pacific

* CCC yeChina uye chitupa cheJapan che[TELEC](https://www.telec.or.jp/ENG/) zvakakosha kune zvigadzirwa mumatunhu aya.
* Misika iri kusimukira yakaita seIndia inogona kuda [BIS](https://bis.gov.in/) (Bureau of Indian Standards) kana kutevedzera kwakafanana.

Kuyananisa Kwepasi Rose

Masangano epasi rose akaita se[International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC)](https://www.iec.ch/) anogadzira mitemo inoyedza kuenzanisa nzira dzekuyedza, kuderedza kuongororwa kunodzokororwa uye kufambisa kutevedzera kwemiganhu.

## IV. Njodzi neMari dzeKusatevedzera

Kusatevedzera hakusi nyaya yemutemo chete; kuriwo kwekushanda nemari:

* Njodzi dzemutemo: zvirango, kudzorerwa kwezvigadzirwa, kana kurambidzwa mumisika yakakosha.
* Kukanganisa kushanda: kunonoka kwemapurojekiti nekuda kwekuchengetwa netsika.
* Kukanganisa mukurumbira: kukuvara kwemufananidzo kana kuiswa kukatadza kana kutyora mitemo yemuno.

Kune zvikwata zvevatengi, kuongorora zvitupa kunodzivirira kugadzirisa kunodhura mushure mekuiswa.
