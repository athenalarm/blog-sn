### II. Kukurukurirana nenzira dzakawanda & Kutsungirira kweNetiweki

Muna 2026, kukurukurirana nenzira dzakawanda haichisiri fashoni — chava chidikanwi chekushanda kwakavimbika pasirese. Vagoveri vanofanira kukoshesa panera dzekudzora dzinotsigira nzira dzinodarika imwe dzekukurukura dzakadai se GSM cellular uye Wi-Fi.

Nei zvichikosha:
- **Kutsungirira kweNetiweki**: Nzira mbiri dzekukurukura dzinovimbisa kuti maaratamu, kuvandudzwa kwemamiriro, uye kuchinja kwekugadzirisa zvinosvitswa kunyangwe nzira imwe ikatadza kushanda (izvi zvakakosha mumatunhu ane internet kana mbozhanhare isina kusimba).
- **Kusiyana kweZiviso dzeAratamu**: Panera dzinotsigira ziviso dze APP push, mameseji e SMS, uye nhare dzenzwi dzinowedzera mukana wekuti vashandisi vapedzisira vagamuchira ziviso dzakakosha nenguva.

Chirongwa chekutenga che B2B chenyanzvi chinofanira kugara chichitarisa mafrequencies e GSM anotsigirwa (semuenzaniso, 850/900/1800/1900 MHz), mazinga e Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11b/g/n), uye kuchinjika kwezvirongwa zvechaneli kuti zvifambirane nezvinodiwa nevanopa masevhisi emunetiweki mumisika yakanangwa.

### III. Kukwanisa Kukura: Huwandu hweNzvimbo (Zones) & Kutsigira maDetector

Vagoveri vanoda zvigadzirwa zvinokwanisa kukura. Panera yekudzora inokwanisa kushandiswa kudzimba diki, dzimba dzeumbozha (villas), kana zvivakwa zvikuru zvekugara inomhanyisa kutengeswa kwezvinhu pasina kuwedzera huwandu hwe ma SKU.

Paunenge uchiongorora panera dze nheyo dze chengetedzo isina waya dzevatengi vepasirese, nyatsotarisa kune:
- **Huwandu hweNzvimbo (Zone capacity)**: Kutsigira nzvimbo dzinosvika 100 dzisina waya kunobvumira panera imwe yekudzora kuvhara zvivakwa zvakaoma.
- **Kugarisana kwe Detector**: Tsvaga rutsigiro rwakafara rwe PIR motion senza, masenza emagonhi/mawindo, ma detector eutsi, masenza egasi, uye zvimwe zvekubatsira.
- **Mazinga eFrequency & Coding**: Vimbisa kugarisana nemazinga epasirese asina waya akaita se 433 MHz ine EV1527 encoding — sarudzo inogamuchirwa zvakanyanya mumisika yakawanda yepasirese.

Kuchinjika kwakadaro kwakakosha kune vatengesi vanobata mamiriro akasiyana ekugadzira apo panera-imwe-inokwana-vazhinji inovandudza purofiti.

### V. Simba & Kuvimbika kweMamiriro ekunze

Kusimba kwepanera yekudzora ndicho chinhu chekutanga kune vapinzi ve B2B vanofanira kutarisa mamiriro ekunze akasiyana.

Zvinhu zvakakosha zvinosanganisira:
- **Kushandiswa kwesimba kwakaderera**: Inovimbisa hupenyu hurefu hwekugara yakagadzirira (standby) uye kugadziriswa kusingawanzoitiki.
- **Mabhatiri emukati ekubatsira (Backup batteries)**: Inochengetedza panobva magetsi makuru — inova nzvimbo huru yekukundikana mumatunhu mamwe.
- **Nzvimbo yekushandira (Operating range)**: Panera dzakagadzirirwa kupisa kwakasiyana uye mwando (<80% mwando usina kuomesa, tembiricha dzekushanda kubva 0–55°C kana kupfuura) dzinoshanda zvakavimbika mumamiriro ekunze akasiyana.

Kusimba kunoshanduka kuita kutadza kushanda kushoma munharaunda, kudzoserwa kwezvinhu kushoma, uye mukurumbira wakasimba wemugoveri mumisika yakaoma.
